Self-Retracting Devices (SRDs)

Protecta SRDs are drum-wound wire rope lifelines that extend and retract out of a metal or thermoplastic housing. When a fall occurs, a built-in locking mechanism activates to arrest the user and stop their descent. Protecta SRDs are heavy-duty fall arresters secured to a mounting point, available for the user to connect to when work begins.

SRDs for Leading Edge (SRD-LEs)

Protecta SRD-LEs function similarly to Protecta SRDs, but SRD-LEs include functionality for working on or near sharp edges and abrasive surfaces. The lifeline is strenghtened to resist fraying and cutting, enabling the user to work in even the most challenging of environments.

SRDs for Rescue (SRD-Rs)

Protecta SRD-Rs are specially designed for use in Rescue applications. Protecta SRD-Rs include a crank that controls lifeline payout, enabling the user to raise or lower a suspended worker during rescue. In addition to Rescue applications, SRD-Rs may be used for Fall Arrest and Restraint applications in the same way as standard SRDs.

Personal Self-Retracting Devices

Personal SRDs are light-weight SRDs that are mounted to the user's back, as opposed to a mounting point. Personal SRDs are easy to carry and enable the user to quickly travel between different worksites and connection points. Personal SRDs include the Twin SRD variant, which pairs two Personal SRDs for maintaining 100-percent tie-off during transfers.
