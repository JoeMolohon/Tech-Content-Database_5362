Product Specifications

Retracted Length 20.5 in. (52 cm)

Lifeline Type Stainless steel cable
Product Weight 10 lb. (4.5 kg)
User Capacity 310 lb. (140 kg)

Product Description

Personal SRDs are light-weight SRDs that are mounted to the user's back, as opposed to a mounting point. Personal SRDs are easy to carry and enable the user to quickly travel between different worksites and connection points. Personal SRDs include the Twin SRD variant, which pairs two Personal SRDs for maintaining 100-percent tie-off during transfers.
