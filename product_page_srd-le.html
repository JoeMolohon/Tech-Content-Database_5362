Product Specifications

Retracted Length 20.5 in. (52 cm)
Extended Length 60 ft. (18 m)
Lifeline Type Stainless steel cable
Product Weight 10 lb. (4.5 kg)
User Capacity 310 lb. (140 kg)

Product Description

Protecta SRD-LEs function similarly to Protecta SRDs, but SRD-LEs include functionality for working on or near sharp edges and abrasive surfaces. The lifeline is strenghtened to resist fraying and cutting, enabling the user to work in even the most challenging of environments.
